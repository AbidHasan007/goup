আমাদের ইসকুল

The GO UP Foundation has always strived to bring underprivileged children into the mainstream through a variety of projects, including education, food, and sports. One of the projects of the Go Up Foundation "আমাদের ইসকুল" has started its official activities in the Bahadurshah Park Area. In the implementation of SDG Goal 3,4,16 Today we officially conduct our school activities with about 20 underprivileged children Those who are unable to start their studies due to a financial project. In addition to studies, technical education, hand work, crafting, physical education will be provided in "আমাদের ইসকুল" project.
